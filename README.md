## 🔍 Key Insights  

### 👤 User Risk  
- Fraud heavily concentrated in **new users (≤30 days)**  
- Indicates **fake account / onboarding abuse**

### 💳 Payment Risk  
- Prepaid (24) & Credit Card (17) dominate fraud  
- Bank transfers → near-zero fraud  

### 🌍 Geographic Risk  
- Fraud is **clustered**, not random  
- High concentration in **India & Australia**

### ⚠️ Behavioral Signals  
- Refund spikes (max = 4)  
- Failed payments peak (15)  
➡️ Strong indicator of **card testing fraud**

### 🌐 Device & IP Patterns  
- Top IP → **21 fraud events**  
- Repeated devices across transactions  
➡️ Suggests **coordinated fraud activity**

## 🧩 Risk Scoring Framework  
Built rule-based model using:  
- Refund ratio & count  
- Failed transactions  
- Device switching  
- Login variance  

**Output:**  
- 🟢 Low Risk: 95.02%  
- 🟡 Medium Risk: 4.98%  
- 🔴 High Risk: Flagged via combined indicators
